Integrates Getepay with Contact Form 7. Start accepting payment with Contact Form 7 & Getepay today.

== Description ==
This is a gateway extension for Contact Form 7 plugin to use Getepay Payment Gateway.

Payments are processed offsite at [Getepay](https://getepay.in) and the customer will be redirected back to your site after completing the payment.

== Installation ==

= Automatic Installation =
> 1. Sign in to your WordPress site as an administrator.
> 2. In the main menu go to Plugins -> Add New.
> 3. Search for Getepay for Contact Form 7 and click install.
> 4. That's it. You are now ready to start accepting Getepay payment on your website through your contact form.
